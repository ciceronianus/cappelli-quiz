Cappelli-Quiz

Do you think that you are quite good at Latin abbreviations? That you can read them fluently? Or are you currently learning them? If you dare, try this Cappelli-Quiz based on the famous dictionary of abbreviations.

As soon as you click Start quiz, you will get 10 abbreviations.
Try to solve them in any order. When you have all of them, you will get a score.

This little silly Quiz uses publicly available data from Cappelli Online that was produced via crowdsourcing. Look at that project, it is great!
